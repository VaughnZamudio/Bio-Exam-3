# Bio 115 Flashcard Deck

A sleek, mobile-responsive study tool designed for Bio 115 Exam 3 preparation. This interactive web application helps users master biology concepts across multiple systems using a prioritized learning algorithm.

**Link:** [vaughnzamudio.github.io/Bio-Exam-3/](https://vaughnzamudio.github.io/Bio-Exam-3/)

---

## 🧬 Featured Study Systems
The deck is organized into seven core biological modules:
* **Development:** Human development, fertilization, and aging.
* **Chromosomes:** Cell cycles, mitosis, meiosis, and chromosomal disorders.
* **Genetics:** Inheritance patterns, Punnett squares, and genetic diseases.
* **DNA:** Biology, technology, replication, and CRISPR.
* **Cancer:** Characteristics, causes, and treatments.
* **Ecology:** Ecosystems, energy flow, and global environmental cycles.
* **Evolution:** Natural selection, human evolution, and fossil evidence.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Interactive Flashcards:** Tap to flip cards and reveal detailed answers and memory hints.
* **Progress Tracking:** Mark cards as "Got it" or "Still learning" to automatically track your mastery.
* **Smart Filtering:** Study all cards at once, or focus specifically on "Need work" or "Unseen" cards.
* **System Progress:** Visual progress bars for each individual biology system to identify your weak spots.
* **Touch & Gesture Optimized:** Swipe left or right to navigate between cards on mobile devices.
* **Keyboard Shortcuts:** Study faster on desktop using `Space` (flip), `Arrows` (navigate), `K` (known), and `L` (learn).

## 🛠️ How to Use
1.  **Select a System:** Use the horizontal scroll bar at the top to filter by a specific topic.
2.  **Choose a Mode:** Use "All cards," "Need work," or "Unseen" to manage your study session.
3.  **Study:** Flip the card to check your knowledge. Use the "Memory hints" for difficult concepts.
4.  **Mark Progress:** Categorize the card to update your statistics.
5.  **Review:** Use the "Deck tools" to view lists of cards you've mastered or need to revisit.

## 💻 Technologies Used
* **HTML5/CSS3:** Custom UI with a dark-mode "glassmorphism" aesthetic and CSS 3D transforms.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** Logic for deck shuffling, filtering, and state management.
* **Local Storage:** Your study progress is saved locally in your browser, so you can pick up where you left off.
* **Google Fonts:** Utilizing *DM Serif Display*, *DM Sans*, and *DM Mono* for a clean, academic look.
